destroy

Destroy an App in the Boxfuse Console. This will kill all current deployments of this app in all environments, remove all its images from the Boxfuse Vault, and destroy the app in the Boxfuse Console.

Usage: boxfuse destroy app

> boxfuse destroy hello

Destroying myuser/hello ...
Successfully destroyed myuser/hello

Machine-readable output (sample)

{"success":"true"}
