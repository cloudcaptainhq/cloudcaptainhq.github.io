Both last year and this year there have been a large number of Boxfuse events with many more to come!

In March, Boxfuse founder and CEO, Axel Fontaine spoke about Immutable Infrastructure: Rise of the Machine Images at QCon London 2016. This talk details the principles and the lessons learned Boxfuse is based upon. And it was a huge success with over 400 attendees in the room who gave it excellent reviews.
